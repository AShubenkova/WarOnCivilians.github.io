About the Project

This research project investigates the spread of civilian suppression techniques across countries in the early 20th century.

It is funded by an ERC Advanced Grant and brings together historians, political scientists, and data experts from multiple institutions.

The project runs from 2025 to 2029 and aims to produce open-access datasets and publications.
